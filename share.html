FAQs

Questions people might ask you, and how to answer them. Tap to expand.

"Who is this for?"

Mid-career professionals (5+ years experience) who are job searching, recently laid off, thinking about a change, or stuck. Works across industries: tech, finance, healthcare, nonprofit, marketing, operations, government, education. Canada and US. Both employed and unemployed.

"How much does it cost?"

$2,497 to pay in full, or 3 monthly payments of $899. There's also Klarna financing for up to 12 months. Anyone you refer gets 10% off.

"Is it worth it?"

Izzy's clients average $10K-$25K in salary gains. The average job search takes 5-6 months. Every month searching is $5K-$15K in lost earnings. If the program helps you land even one month faster, it pays for itself multiple times over. And that's before factoring in the salary increase.

"What if they're currently employed?"

Half of Izzy's clients are employed. The program is designed for busy professionals: one call per week, everything else on your schedule. The AI toolkit means things move fast without requiring tons of hours.

"What makes this different from other career coaches?"

Most career coaches give generic advice and a template resume. Izzy builds everything custom, powered by AI tools that do in 2 hours what used to take 20. Then he coaches on strategy, not just deliverables. You get 10+ custom assets, a full strategy, and 8 weeks of live coaching. A typical resume writer charges $300-$1,500 for one document.

"What if they don't land a job in 8 weeks?"

The program builds a system that keeps working beyond 8 weeks. Some clients land in week 2, some in week 10. The resume, LinkedIn, network, and skills last. The community access doesn't disappear.

"Can they join from outside Toronto?"

Yes. The program is fully virtual. Anyone in Canada or the US can join. The only in-person component is an optional VIP retreat in late May.

"What's the time commitment?"

One group call per week (60-90 minutes). Everything else is async. The AI toolkit handles the heavy lifting. Most clients spend 3-5 hours per week total.

"What if they've tried a career coach before and it didn't work?"

Izzy's approach is different. He builds every asset custom (not templates), uses AI to do deep personalized research, and coaches on strategy and positioning. There's a reason 98% of his clients rate him 5 stars.
