Tech, finance, healthcare, nonprofit, government, marketing, operations, supply chain, higher education. If you're a professional with 5+ years of experience, this works. The system adapts to your industry, not the other way around.

One group call per week (60-90 minutes). Everything else is async and self-paced. The AI toolkit handles the heavy lifting on deliverables, so you're not spending 20 hours writing a resume. Most clients spend 3-5 hours per week total.

"How is this different from LinkedIn Premium or a resume writer?"
